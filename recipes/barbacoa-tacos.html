Barbacoa Tacos

Details

This easy recipe utilizes flavor packed ingredients added into a pressure cooker to make the most delicious barbacoa beef you've ever had! Throw the beef with a couple toppings of your choice into a taco to experience a little slice of heaven in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup beer
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 chipotles in adobo sauce
  • 1 small white onion, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 (4oz) can chopped green chiles
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 tbls apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbls ground cumin
  • 1 tbls dried Mexican oregano
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbls olive oil
  • 3 lbs beef chuck roast, cut into 2 in chunks
  • 3 bay leaves

Directions

  1. Turn on the "Saute" settings of your InstantPot and add oil. Once heated, add in the roast and sear on all sides. Then turn off the InstantPot.
  2. Add all the rest of the ingredients to the pot. Close the lid and set it to "Sealing."
  3. Turn your InstantPot on to "Pressure" on high pressure for 60 min.
  4. Once the timer goes off, carefully switch the lid's valve to "Venting" and wait for all of the steam to be released.
  5. Remove the lid and discard bay leaves and chipotles.
  6. Shred the beef into your desired size. Save some of the juices for the beef to soak up, especially if you plan on saving some in the fridge.
  7. Serve up on a toasted tortilla with your favorite fresh toppings and enjoy!
